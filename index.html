About Riposte

Riposte is a fast-paced top-down battle arena focused on competitive PvP combat.
Riposte emulates the exciting team fights and battles from games like DOTA and League of Legends without being bound by long game times or excessive number of items and abilites.

Riposte achieves this by stripping away the long periods of farming and the complex item builds and adding in more action-based game modes thus, making the game feel faster. With a smaller learning curve yet the same high skill cap. Riposte is ideal for MOBA fans looking for a light-weight yet engaging experience.

Latest Updates

Alpha 2.0

8 November 2017

This Alpha contains all the base content of Riposte, fully realized with up to 5v5 combat.

We are excited to announce that we have finally implemented multiplayer into Riposte. Players can now Create or Join lobbies with up to 9 other friends and battle it out in Best of Five Team Deathmatch. Fans of singleplayer can now enjoy a more intelligent AI and can also select the number of AI they wish to fight with/against.

An all new soundtrack and remastered sound effects help increase immersion and put you right into the action. There has also been a complete overhaul of the UI and game menu to provide a much more polished experince. Players can also now enable Quick Cast, Mouse Acceleration and adjust Volume Settings.

Stay tuned for various bug fixes and the announcements of new characters and game modes.

Alpha 1.1

19 October 2017

This improvement on Alpha 1.0 contained 80% of the base content for Riposte

New Sound Effects, Music, Particle Effects and a new Map Redesign help bring this build to life. Players now have the use of the new Reflect Ability to deflect oncoming projecticles.

Players now have the opportunity to go head-to-head against an intelligent AI which seeks out and enganges players.

Alpha 1.0

15 September 2017

This Alpha was a successful proof of concept demo for Riposte.

This build contains the central mechanics of the game and the handcrafted Battle Arena. Currently players can move around the arena with the character being able to navigate around objects by itself. Players could use the Blink, Shoot and Melee abilites complete with animations and particle effects.
