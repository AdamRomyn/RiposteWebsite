About Riposte

Riposte is a fast-paced top-down battle arena focused on competitive PvP combat. The game aims to emulate the exciting team fights and battles from games like DOTA and League of Legends without being bound by long game times.

Riposte achieves this by stripping away the long periods of farming and the complex item builds and adding in more action-based game modes. Thus, making the game feel faster with a smaller learning curve while still maintain the same high skill cap. Characters will have simple low-cooldown abilities, low hit points and there will be no extra or purchasable items.
